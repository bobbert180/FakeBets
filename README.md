# FakeBets

FakeBets is an app designed to help you track your betting activity over a period of time. The goal is to answer the age-old question: should you really have been placing those bets, or does the house always win in the end?

## Overview

With FakeBets, you can simulate your betting strategies without risking real money. By logging your hypothetical bets, outcomes, and strategies, you gain valuable insights into your betting habits and long-term results. This allows you to objectively analyze whether your approach would have been profitable or if the odds are truly stacked against you.

## Key Features

- **Track Bets:** Log each bet with details such as date, event, odds, stake, and outcome.
- **Simulate Strategies:** Test different betting strategies and see how they perform over time.
- **Analyze Results:** Visualize your performance with charts and statistics, including win/loss ratios, ROI, and streaks.
- **No Real Money Required:** All bets are simulated, so you can experiment risk-free.
- **Insights & Reports:** Generate reports to review your betting history and identify patterns or areas for improvement.

## Why Use FakeBets?

- **Risk-Free Learning:** Perfect for beginners who want to learn about betting without financial risk.
- **Strategy Testing:** Experienced bettors can refine their strategies before committing real money.
- **Data-Driven Decisions:** Make informed choices based on historical data and trends.
